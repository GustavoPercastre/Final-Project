This is our Project

We have been working on a brand new Game!

Fallout 76 will be a online roleplaying game.

It is set in an alternate history, and takes place in 2102, twenty-five years after a nuclear war that devastated the Earth. The player character is a resident of Vault 76, a fallout shelter that was built in West Virginia to house America's best and brightest minds.

We will be taking Fallout 4 mechanics and building upon them.

This game will allow you to travel the wastelands with friends, and build settlements to try and survive the Nuclear Apocalypse.
